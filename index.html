About Me

Hello! My name is Donit Gashi.
I come from a beautiful and newly independent country located in the South Western part of Europe called Kosovo. I am currently living and studying in Germany. I study Computer Science at International University (IU) in Bad Honnef. I am also employed full-time as a Graphic Designer from 2019.
I have been facinated about computers and technology from the moment I got my first computer and gaming console. Some of my interests are software development and design, web and application development, artifical intelligence, machine learning and robotics. Further, I am also eager to learn new programming languages and skills needed to create and design websites. My job experience has helped me further develop my skills and enrich my creativity. As a Graphic Designer, I am passionate about helping businesses and individuals develop their brand and use graphics to communicate with their audience.
In the future, I would like to develop applications and websites that will make life easier for people.

Experience

Intership at Xhad Studio (2018-2019)

XHAD is a graphic design studio located in Prishtina, Kosovo. Throughout my internship in XHAD, I had the opportunity to understand what are the roles and responsibilities of a graphic designer and develop skills needed to pursue this career. Since my position included working with several members in a team, I developed my communication skills and also got a first hand experience on team work and support. Moreover, I developed my critical thinking and creativity since I had to assist in project designsing e.g. marketing brochures, website, posters, branding, layouts etc.

Graphic designer at N.T.P KGT (2019-ongoing)

N.T.P KGT is a company that offer printing services e.g. books, flyers, posters, etc., located in Prishtina, Kosovo. As a graphic designer at KGT my responsibilities are mainly to discuss concepts and ideas with clients and create designs. I create designs for book covers, catalogues, posters, logos, brochures. Other than that, I oversee the printing process of each of the products, e.g. books or catalogues. Throughout this experience, I had the opportunity to further develop my communication, leadership, team working, creativity and so forth.
